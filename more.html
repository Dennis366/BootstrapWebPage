Activities

Northern Lights Tour

Witness the breathtaking Aurora Borealis in the Arctic skies of Lapland. Join an expert guide on a magical evening tour through snowy landscapes, cozy camps, and quiet wilderness spots where the lights appear most vividly. Learn about the science and folklore behind this natural wonder while sipping warm drinks by a campfire, making it an unforgettable Arctic experience.

Dog Sledding

Experience the thrill of riding a team of energetic Siberian Huskies through the snowy trails of Santa Claus Village. Guided by expert mushers, you'll glide past magical winter scenery, cozy forests, and frozen rivers, feeling the rush of the Arctic winds. This unforgettable adventure is perfect for all ages and captures the true spirit of Lapland's winter magic.

Ice Hotel

The Icehotel in Jukkasjärvi is known to be the biggest hotel of ice and snow in the world, spanning over some 6,000 square metres (64,600 sq ft). Each suite is unique, and the architecture of the hotel is changed each year as it is rebuilt from scratch.

Each year, artists submit their ideas for suites, and a jury selects about 50 artists to create the church, Icebar, reception, main hall, and suites. When spring comes, everything melts away and returns to the Torne River.

The Icehotel only exists between December and April, and has been listed as one of the Seven Wonders of Sweden.
